## Architectural style

- Eastlake Stick Victorian, dating to the first wave of Hayes Valley mansion
  building in 1885.
- Architect and builder attribution: Berkeley Heritage identifies 289-291 Page
  Street (1885) as a William H. Wharff house, with Wharff serving as both
  designer and contractor.
- Some details likely ordered from a period millwork catalog, consistent with
  Eastlake Movement construction practice.
